# Risk of Rain 2 (2020) — Hopoo Games

> **Genre:** Third-person 3D Action Roguelite | **Perspective:** Third-person 3D | **Players:** 1–4 Online Co-op | **Steam Rating:** 95% Positive

## 🎮 Core Concept

Survive on an alien planet by looting items, activating teleporters, defeating bosses, and escaping — or looping to fight again harder. Items stack infinitely, creating exponential power growth.

## 📦 Item System — The Core Innovation

### Item Tiers

| Tier          | Color        | Drop Rate         | Stack Behavior              |
| ------------- | ------------ | ----------------- | --------------------------- |
| **Common**    | White        | Most frequent     | Linear stacking             |
| **Uncommon**  | Green        | Moderate          | Linear/diminishing          |
| **Legendary** | Red          | Rare              | Powerful unique effects     |
| **Boss**      | Yellow       | Boss drops only   | Unique boss-kill rewards    |
| **Lunar**     | Blue         | Lunar Pods / shop | Risk/reward tradeoffs       |
| **Void**      | Purple (DLC) | Corrupted items   | Replace existing items      |
| **Equipment** | Orange       | Active use        | 1 at a time, cooldown-based |

### Key Items (Examples of Stacking Power)

- **Soldier's Syringe** (White) — +15% attack speed per stack → 57 = near instant attacks
- **Ukulele** (Green) — 25% chance to chain lightning → multiple stacks = constant chains
- **Brilliant Behemoth** (Red) — All attacks explode for 60% damage
- **57 Leaf Clover** (Legendary) — All RNG rolls twice, takes better result
- **Shaped Glass** (Lunar) — Double damage, halve health (stacks!)

### RNG Mitigation: Printers & Shrines

- **3D Printers** — Sacrifice existing items to print copies of a specific item
- **Cleansing Pool** — Convert Lunar items to Pearls
- **Shrine of Order** — Converts all items to random equal stacks (gamble!)
- **Scrapper** — Break items into scrap for Printers

## ⏱️ Dynamic Difficulty Scaling

- **Difficulty timer** runs constantly — enemies scale with time AND stage
- Creates tension: explore for items vs. rush to stay ahead of scaling
- **Difficulty indicator bar** shows current level (Easy → HAHAHA)
- **Looping** — After Stage 5, loop back with dramatically harder enemies
- Co-op scales enemy HP and spawn rates per player count

## 🗺️ Stage Structure

```
Stage 1 (4 variants) → Stage 2 (4 variants) → Stage 3 (3 variants) →
Stage 4 (2 variants) → Stage 5 (Moon/Commencement) →
  ├─ Escape (victory)
  ├─ Loop (restart at Stage 1, harder)
  └─ Void Locus → Planetarium (DLC final boss)
```

## 🌟 Key Innovations

1. **Infinite Item Stacking** — Same items stack for exponential power; "dare you to break it"
2. **2D → 3D Transition** — Bold leap from pixel 2D to full 3D without losing identity
3. **Time-Based Difficulty** — Creates constant urgency and risk/reward decisions
4. **Visual Item Display** — All items visibly attached to character model
5. **RNG Mitigation (Printers)** — Turn bad RNG into strategic decisions
6. **Artifact System** — 16 gameplay modifiers (e.g., enemies drop items on kill, friendly fire)
7. **Eclipse/Typhoon Difficulty** — 8 levels of difficulty modifiers per survivor
