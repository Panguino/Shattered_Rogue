# Into the Breach (2018) — Subset Games

> **Genre:** Tactical Roguelike | **Perspective:** Isometric 2D Grid | **Players:** Solo | **Steam Rating:** 93% Positive

## 🎮 Core Concept

Command a squad of 3 mechs from the future to defend cities from giant bug-like Vek. Turn-based tactical combat on 8×8 grids where you can see enemy actions before they happen — every move is a puzzle.

## ⚔️ Core Mechanic — Perfect Information

- **Enemies show their next move** before your turn
- Every turn is a puzzle: How do I prevent all damage to buildings?
- Mechs can push, pull, block, and redirect enemies
- Protecting buildings is priority — buildings = power grid = HP

## 📈 Progression

- **Pilot XP** — Pilots level up and can be carried between timelines
- **Coins** — Earn from achievements, buy new squads
- **No stat inflation** — Pure strategic mastery
- **Achievements** unlock new squads and pilots

## 🌟 Key Innovation

**Perfect information + roguelike** — You know exactly what will happen; the challenge is optimizing your response. Chess meets roguelike.
