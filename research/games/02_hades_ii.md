# Hades II (2024 Early Access) — Supergiant Games

> **Genre:** Action Roguelite | **Perspective:** Isometric 3D | **Players:** Solo | **Status:** Early Access

## 🎮 Core Concept

Play as Melinoë, Princess of the Underworld and Zagreus's sister. A witch who must defeat Chronos, the Titan of Time, who has seized the Underworld.

## 🔫 Weapons

6+ weapons with Aspects:

- **Witch's Staff** — Versatile melee/ranged staff
- **Sister Blades** — Fast dual daggers
- **Moonstone Axe** — Heavy, slow power hits
- **Umbral Flames** — Medium-range flame attacks
- **Argent Skull** — Thrown projectile weapon
- **Black Coat** — Scythe-like weapon (added in updates)

## ✨ New Mechanics (vs. Hades 1)

### Magick System & Omega Moves

- **Magick Bar** — Secondary resource that fuels powerful "Omega" versions of attacks/specials/casts
- **Hold to Charge** — Pressing and holding ability buttons charges Omega versions
- Player manages health AND magick simultaneously

### Arcana Cards

- Tarot-like system of equippable buffs
- Each card has a "Grasp" cost — limited slots force strategic choices
- Unlocked with Ashes currency
- Examples: increased dash distance, bonus damage on first hit, extra lives

### Incantations (Cauldron Crafting)

- Replace Hades 1's House Contractor
- Use gathered resources (flowers, metals, bones, monster parts) to perform rituals
- Unlock: new gameplay systems, NPC appearances, game mechanics, tool upgrades

### Sprint (Replaces Multi-Dash)

- Melinoë has an endless sprint instead of Zagreus's quick dashes
- Sprint can be buffed with Boons for speed/damage
- Fundamentally changes movement feel — more fluid, less staccato

### Familiar System

- Animal companions that accompany runs
- Block projectiles, grant extra lives, assist with resource gathering
- Frinos the frog, Toula the cat, others

### Testament of the Night

- Hades II's version of the Pact of Punishment
- Custom difficulty modifiers for increased challenge
