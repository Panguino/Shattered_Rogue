### Weapon Evolution System (Key Innovation)

- Max level a weapon (Level 8) + hold matching passive item = Evolution
- Chest after boss drops evolved weapon
- Evolution is massive power spike + visual change
- Example: King Bible (Lv8) + Spellbinder = Unholy Vespers

### Weapon Slots

- **6 weapon slots** — fill strategically
- **6 passive item slots** — pair with correct weapons for evolutions

## 📈 Progression Systems

### In-Run

- XP gems from kills → Level up → Choose 1 of 3-4 weapon/item offers
- Reach level 8 on a weapon to evolve (with matching passive)
- Treasure chests from bosses (10-min intervals)
- Floor chicken (healing), vacuum (XP magnet), gems
