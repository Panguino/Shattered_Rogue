# Deep Rock Galactic: Survivor (2024 EA) — Funday Games / Ghost Ship Publishing

> **Genre:** 2D Survivor Roguelite | **Perspective:** Top-down 2D | **Players:** Solo | **Steam Rating:** 86% Positive

## 🎮 Core Concept

A survivor-like roguelite set in the Deep Rock Galactic universe. Choose a dwarf class, mine resources, fight waves of bugs, and build chaotic overpowered loadouts. Combines Vampire Survivors' auto-attack loop with DRG's mining and class identity.

## 🔫 Combat System

- **Auto-attack** — Weapons fire automatically (VS-style)
- **Aiming** — Weapons target nearest/strongest enemy
- **Overclocks** — Run-specific weapon modifications (powerful modifiers)
- **Artifact System** — Passive buffs collected during runs
- **Mining** — Actively mine resources during combat for crafting/upgrades

## ⛏️ Mining as Gameplay (Key Innovation)

- Resources spawn in the arena
- Mine them DURING combat for minerals, nitra, gold
- Minerals power upgrades and unlock crafting
- Creates dual-focus gameplay: fight + mine simultaneously
- True to DRG identity

## 📈 Progression

### In-Run

- XP gems → Level up → Choose weapons/upgrades/overclocks
- Mine resources → Mid-run purchasing/crafting
- Artifacts provide passive bonuses

### Meta-Progression
