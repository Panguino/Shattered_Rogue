# Enter the Gungeon (2016) — Dodge Roll Games

> **Genre:** Bullet-hell Top-down Roguelike | **Perspective:** Top-down 2D | **Players:** 1–2 Local Co-op | **Steam Rating:** 95% Positive

## 🎮 Core Concept

Descend into the Gungeon — a dungeon made of, about, and for guns — to find the legendary gun that can kill the past. Bullet-hell dodge-rolling combat with an absurd variety of creative firearms.

### Notable Guns

- **Casey** — A baseball bat. Hit bullets back at enemies
- **The Kiln** — Shoots pots that break into pottery shards
- **Mailbox** — Delivers mail (damage)
- **Gunther** — A gun that talks and evolves during the run
- **Makeshift Cannon** — Single shot that kills almost anything, then becomes empty

### Synergy System

- 330+ unique synergies between gun + item combinations
- Synergy creates visual and mechanical changes
- Example: Any fire weapon + Ring of Fire Resistance = no self-damage
- Gunslinger character: EVERY gun has active synergies

## 🧰 Items — Passive + Active

### Passive Items (250+)

- **Ser Junkan** — Junk companion that evolves (stronger per junk collected)
- **Riddle of Lead** — Can survive fatal hits
- **Duct Tape** — Literally tape two guns together

### Active Items (100+)

- **Blank** — Clear all bullets on screen (limited uses per floor)
- **Teleporter** — Random room teleport
- **Decoy** — Draws enemy attention

## 🗺️ Floor Structure

```
Floor 1: Keep of the Lead Lord → Boss
Floor 2: Gungeon Proper → Boss
Floor 3: Black Powder Mine → Boss
Floor 4: Hollow → Boss
Floor 5: Forge → Dragun (final boss)
  ├─ Bullet Hell (secret Floor 6) → Lich (true final boss)
  └─ Individual pasts (per character)
```

- Secret floors: Oubliette, Abbey of the True Gun, Resourceful Rat lair

### Hegemony Credits (Meta Currency)

- Earned by killing bosses without taking damage ("Master Round")
- Spent to unlock weapons and items at the shop NPCs
- More NPCs rescued = more items available

## 🌟 Key Innovations

1. **Dodge Roll** — Signature mechanic — iframe rolls through bullets, table flips for cover
2. **Table Flipping** — Kick tables to create bulletproof cover, then kick them at enemies
3. **Absurd Gun Variety** — Over 300 creative/joke weapons (gun that shoots guns)
4. **Synergy System** — 330+ hidden gun+item combos change behavior
5. **Master Rounds** — Perfect boss fights = permanent HP upgrade (high skill reward)
6. **NPC Rescue Pool Expansion** — Metagame of unlocking by rescuing NPCs
