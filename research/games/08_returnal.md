# Returnal (2021) — Housemarque

> **Genre:** Third-person 3D Bullet-hell Roguelite | **Perspective:** Third-person 3D | **Players:** 1–2 Online Co-op | **Steam Rating:** 85% Positive

## 🎮 Core Concept

Selene, an astronaut, crash-lands on alien planet Atropos and is trapped in a death loop. Fast-paced, bullet-hell-inspired third-person shooting with a deep psychological horror narrative. Each death resets the procedurally generated world.

### Weapon Trait System (Key Innovation)

- Each weapon has **10+ unlockable traits** per weapon type
- Traits modify weapon behavior: homing bullets, explosive rounds, portal fire, etc.
- Traits unlock permanently by using the weapon in combat (proficiency)
- Higher proficiency = access to Trait Level II and III (stronger versions)
- Each weapon drops with 1–3 active traits randomly selected
- Creates massive variety — same weapon type can play completely differently

### Alt-Fire Modes

- Every weapon has a unique **Alt-Fire** (adaptive trigger half-press = primary, full press = alt-fire on PS5)
- Examples: Killsight (precision scope), Trackerswarm (homing missiles), Shockstream (chain lightning)

## 🦠 Artifact & Parasite System

### Artifacts (Passive Bonuses)

- Collected items that provide pure bonuses
- Examples: +25% melee damage, resin counter for max integrity, protection bonus

### Parasites (Risk/Reward)

| Parasite  | Benefit                        | Drawback                     |
| --------- | ------------------------------ | ---------------------------- |
| Example 1 | +20% damage vs. low-HP enemies | -10% max integrity           |
| Example 2 | Increased pickup radius        | Reduced repair efficiency    |
| Example 3 | Enemies drop extra obolites    | Increased malfunction chance |

Parasites always have a trade-off — attach for benefit, suffer the drawback.

### Malfunctions (Debuffs)

- Triggered by picking up Malignant items (purple glowing items = risky)
- Must complete a task to clear malfunction (e.g., kill 3 enemies in 10s, open 2 chests)
- Examples: Lose obolites per hit, melee deals no damage, enemies become faster

## 📈 Progression Systems

### In-Run

- **Weapons + Traits** — Escalating weapon proficiency
- **Artifacts** — Passive bonuses
- **Parasites** — Risk/reward modifiers
- **Consumables** — Single-use items
- **Silphium (health) / Resin (max HP)** — Health management

### Meta-Progression (Minimal by Design)

| System                   | Persistence       | Notes                                               |
| ------------------------ | ----------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| **Weapon Trait Unlocks** | Permanent         | Use weapon to unlock traits forever                 |
| **Permanent Equipment**  | Story-gated       | Sword, grapple hook, etc. (open new paths)          |
| **Cthonos**              | Slowly adds items | Device in hub that adds new items to loot pool      |
| **Ether**                | Persists on death | Used to cleanse Malignant items or activate Cthonos |
| **Story Progress**       | Permanent         | Cutscenes, house sequences, lore                    |

## 🌍 Biome Structure — 6 Biomes

```
Act 1: Overgrown Ruins → Crimson Wastes → Derelict Citadel (boss: Nemesis)
Act 2: Echoing Ruins → Fractured Wastes → Abyssal Scar (boss: Ophion)
```

Each biome procedurally assembles from handcrafted room segments.

## 🌟 Key Innovations

1. **3D Bullet Hell** — Translated 2D bullet-hell patterns to 3D third-person (industry first at this scale)
2. **Weapon Trait Progression** — Permanent trait unlocks create long-term weapon evolution
3. **DualSense Integration** — Adaptive triggers for primary/alt-fire; haptic feedback for rain, impacts
4. **Parasite Risk/Reward** — Forced trade-offs on pickups
5. **Malfunction System** — Debuffs as challenges to overcome
6. **SSD Instant Loading** — Near-zero load times after death
7. **3D Audio** — Spatial sound critical for dodging in bullet-hell
