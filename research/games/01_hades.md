# Hades (2020) — Supergiant Games

> **Genre:** Action Roguelite | **Perspective:** Isometric 3D | **Players:** Solo | **Steam Rating:** 98% Positive (138K+ reviews)

## 🎮 Core Concept

Play as Zagreus, son of Hades, fighting through the Underworld to escape to the surface. Death returns you to the House of Hades where the narrative progresses.

## 🔫 Weapons — The Infernal Arms

6 weapons, each with **4 Aspects** that fundamentally change playstyle:

| Weapon            | Type          | Aspects                            | Playstyle                     |
| ----------------- | ------------- | ---------------------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| Stygian Blade     | Melee Sword   | Zagreus, Nemesis, Poseidon, Arthur | Fast combos, AoE specials     |
| Heart-Seeking Bow | Ranged Bow    | Zagreus, Chiron, Hera, Rama        | Charged shots, spread fire    |
| Shield of Chaos   | Melee/Ranged  | Zagreus, Zeus, Chaos, Beowulf      | Block + throw, defensive play |
| Eternal Spear     | Melee Polearm | Zagreus, Achilles, Hades, Guan Yu  | Mid-range pokes, spin attack  |
| Adamant Rail      | Ranged Gun    | Zagreus, Eris, Hestia, Lucifer     | Rapid fire, grenade launcher  |
| Twin Fists        | Melee Fists   | Zagreus, Talos, Demeter, Gilgamesh | Fast close-range combos       |

## 🏛️ Boon System (God Powers)

10 Olympian Gods offer Boons that modify abilities:

- **Zeus** — Lightning chain damage, Jolted status
- **Poseidon** — Knockback, bonus room rewards
- **Ares** — Blade Rift (AoE), Doom (delayed damage)
- **Athena** — Deflect projectiles, reduced damage
- **Aphrodite** — High % damage buff, Weak status
- **Artemis** — Critical hit chance, tracking shots
- **Dionysus** — Hangover (DOT poison), slowed enemies
- **Demeter** — Chill (slow), Decay (shatter at 10 stacks)
- **Hermes** — Speed buffs, dodge chance, extra dashes
- **Chaos** — Risk/reward curses → powerful boons after enduring debuffs

### Duo Boons

Combining boons from two gods can create **Duo Boons** — extremely powerful synergies:

- Ares + Athena = **Merciful End** (Doom triggers on deflect)
- Zeus + Poseidon = **Sea Storm** (knockback triggers lightning)
- Artemis + Ares = **Hunting Blades** (Blade Rifts seek enemies)

## 📈 Progression Systems

### In-Run

- **Boons** — God powers chosen at room rewards
- **Poms of Power** — Level up existing boons
- **Daedalus Hammer** — Weapon modifications (2 per run)
- **Charon's Obol** — Buy items at mid-run shop
- **Centaur Hearts** — Max HP increase

### Meta-Progression

| System           | Currency      | Effect                                      |

| Companions       | Ambrosia      | Summon allies in combat                     |

## 🌟 Key Innovations

1. **Narrative Death** — Every death progresses the story. 300K+ words of dialogue, rarely repeated
2. **God Mode** — Increases damage resistance by 2% per death (starts at 20%, caps at 80%). Not a cheat — elegant accessibility
3. **Pact of Punishment** — Voluntary difficulty modifiers for endgame challenge (Heat system)
4. **Relationship System** — Gift Nectar/Ambrosia to build bonds with 25+ characters, unlocking story arcs
5. **Early Access Model** — Released in EA with story additions per update, building community trust
