# FTL: Faster Than Light (2012) — Subset Games

> **Genre:** Space Strategy Roguelike | **Perspective:** Top-down 2D | **Players:** Solo | **Steam Rating:** 95% Positive

## 🎮 Core Concept

Command a spaceship and its crew on a desperate mission to deliver vital intel to the Federation fleet, pursued by the advancing Rebel armada. Manage systems, crew, and resources across procedurally generated sectors.

## 🔫 Weapon Systems

### Weapon Categories

| Category     | Examples                             | Behavior                                           |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------- |
| **Lasers**   | Burst Laser I/II/III, Dual Lasers    | Fires shots, no ammo cost                          |
| **Beams**    | Halberd Beam, Pike Beam, Glaive Beam | Swipe across rooms, penalized by shields           |
| **Missiles** | Artemis, Breach Missile, Hermes      | Bypass shields, cost 1 missile each                |
| **Flak**     | Flak I, Flak II                      | Scatter shots, random accuracy                     |
| **Ions**     | Ion Blast I/II, Heavy Ion            | Disable enemy systems temporarily                  |
| **Bombs**    | Small Bomb, Fire Bomb, Breach Bomb   | Bypass shields, damage systems/crew, cost missiles |

### Weapon Synergy

- Ion weapons disable shields → Beams deal full damage
- Flak strips shields → Burst lasers hit hull
- Fire bombs set fires → oxygen drains → crew dies
- Hacking pulls shields down → Glaive Beam wipes ship

## 🌟 Key Innovations

1. **"Spaceship Captain" Fantasy** — Managing crew, systems, and combat simultaneously
2. **Rebel Fleet Time Pressure** — Constantly pursued; can't explore everything
3. **Pausable Real-Time Combat** — Pause, target, manage crew, unpause — tactical depth
4. **System-Targeting** — Target specific enemy rooms/systems (weapons, shields, engines)
5. **Crew Management** — Position crew for combat, repair, piloting, manning systems
6. **Power Management** — Redistribute reactor power in real-time based on needs
7. **Events as Narrative** — Text-based encounter events with meaningful choices
