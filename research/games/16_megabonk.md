# Megabonk (2024) — Bippinbits

> **Genre:** 3D Survivor Roguelite | **Perspective:** Third-person 3D | **Players:** Solo | **Steam Rating:** 95% Positive

## 🎮 Core Concept

A 3D roguelike survival game blending Vampire Survivors' auto-attack format with Risk of Rain 2's 3D arenas. Quirky cartoonish characters battle endless waves, collecting XP to choose weapon and stat upgrades. Short runs, deep builds, quest-based unlocks.

### Weapon Slots

- Start with **2 weapon slots**
- Unlock additional slots via quests (up to 5+)
- Each slot can hold a different weapon with independent upgrades

## 📗 Tomes (Passive Bonus Books)

Tomes provide permanent passive bonuses for the run:

- Equippable alongside weapons
- Start with limited tome slots, unlock more via quests
- Examples: +Crit Chance, +Movement Speed, +XP Gain, +Damage %

## 📈 Progression Systems

### In-Run

- **XP → Level up** → Choose 1 of 3 random upgrades (weapon/stat/tome)
- **Gold** → Merchant purchases
- **Shrines** → Temporary powerful buffs
- **Chests** → Random item drops

### Meta-Progression: Quest-Based Unlocks

| Unlock Type           | Unlock Method            | Count   |
| --------------------- | ------------------------ | ------- |
| **Characters**        | Complete specific quests | 20      |
| **Weapons**           | Complete specific quests | 29      |
| **Items**             | Complete specific quests | Various |
| **Tomes**             | Complete specific quests | Various |
| **Weapon/Tome Slots** | Complete specific quests | Several |
| **Total Unlockables** | —                        | **94**  |

> **Key Design:** Meta-progression unlocks **content** (new characters, weapons, items) rather than **power**. You don't get stronger between runs — you get more options.
