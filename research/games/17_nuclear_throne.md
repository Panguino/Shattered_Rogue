# Nuclear Throne (2015) — Vlambeer

> **Genre:** Bullet-hell Top-down Roguelike | **Perspective:** Top-down 2D | **Players:** 1–2 Local Co-op | **Steam Rating:** 92% Positive

## 🎮 Core Concept

Mutant survivors fight through post-apocalyptic wastelands in frantic, hectic bullet-hell combat. Tight, responsive controls with a focus on mastery. Features a unique "looping" system where beating the game restarts at higher difficulty.

## 🔁 The Loop System (Key Innovation)

After defeating the final boss (the Nuclear Throne):

- Game doesn't end — you **loop** back to World 1
- All enemies are harder, new enemy variants appear
- Your mutations and loadout carry over
- Each loop increases difficulty dramatically
- Theoretically infinite loops — community record is Loop 100+
- **Leaderboards** track loop count and score

## 🗺️ World Structure

```
Desert (1-1 to 1-3) → Sewers (2-1 to 2-3) → Scrapyard (3-1 to 3-3) →
Crystal Caves (4-1 to 4-3) → Frozen City (5-1 to 5-3) →
Labs (6-1 to 6-3) → Palace (7-1 to 7-3) → Nuclear Throne (boss)
→ LOOP (restart at 1-1, harder) → LOOP 2 → ...
```

## 📈 Progression — Unlock & Mastery

- **Character unlocks** — Reach certain areas/achievements
- **Crown unlocks** — Special modifiers earned through Vaults
- **B-Skin variants** — Alternate character skins from completing loops
- **No permanent power** — Skill-based mastery
- **Daily/Weekly challenges** — Seeded runs for competitive play

## 🏆 Score & Leaderboard System

- Score based on kills, rads collected, areas reached
- **Daily challenges** — Same seed for all players, compete on leaderboards
- **Loop count** — How many times you looped = bragging rights
- **B-Skins** — Cosmetic rewards for difficult achievements

## 🌟 Key Innovations

1. **Loop System** — Beat the game → restart harder. Infinite scaling challenge
2. **Hectic Speed** — Faster than most roguelikes; twitch reflexes required
3. **Destructible Environments** — Walls, cover, everything can be destroyed
4. **Screen Shake & Game Feel** — Vlambeer pioneered "juice" in game feel
5. **Character Diversity** — 12 mutants with radically different mechanics
6. **Crown System** — Run modifiers chosen at crown vaults
