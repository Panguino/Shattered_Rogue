# Everspace (2017) — ROCKFISH Games

> **Genre:** Space Roguelite Shooter | **Perspective:** First-person 3D | **Players:** Solo | **Steam Rating:** 82% Positive

## 🎮 Core Concept

A run-based space roguelite where you pilot a fighter through procedurally generated sectors, dogfighting enemies, scavenging resources, and upgrading your ship. Each death sends you back to start — but with permanent upgrades. Gorgeous visuals with fast-paced arcade-style combat in space.

## 🗺️ Sector-Based Run Structure

```
Sector 1 (3-4 zones) → Sector 2 (3-4 zones) → ... → Sector 7 → Final Boss
```

- Each zone is a procedurally generated area
- Jump between zones within a sector
- Jump drives consume fuel
- Each zone has: enemies, asteroids, stations, derelicts, traders
- Persistent threat pushes you forward (greed vs. survival)

## 📈 Progression — Run-Based + Persistent

### In-Run

- Craft weapon/equipment upgrades at crafting station
- Scavenge resources from destroyed enemies and derelicts
- Trade with traders for weapons/equipment
- Find blueprints for new items

### Meta-Progression (Permanent)

| System                | Currency          | Effect                                              |
| --------------------- | ----------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| **Ship Perks**        | Credits           | Permanent stat upgrades (HP, shields, damage, etc.) |
| **Ship Unlocks**      | Story progression | Unlock Scout and Gunship                            |
| **Blueprint Unlocks** | Found in runs     | New craftable weapons and devices                   |
| **Story Progress**    | Reaching sectors  | Narrative unfolds across runs                       |

## 🌟 Key Innovations

1. **Run-Based Space Combat** — True roguelite loop in a space shooter (not open-world)
2. **Beautiful Space Environments** — Unreal Engine 4 photorealistic nebulae and asteroid fields
3. **Crafting In-Flight** — Gather materials and craft mid-run
4. **Permanent Perk Upgrades** — Invest credits in ship stats between runs
5. **Story Through Death** — Narrative progresses across multiple runs (similar to Hades concept)
6. **3D Space Dogfighting** — Full 6DOF movement in space
