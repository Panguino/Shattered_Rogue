# Crypt of the NecroDancer (2015) — Brace Yourself Games

> **Genre:** Rhythm Roguelike | **Perspective:** Top-down 2D Grid | **Players:** 1–3 Local Co-op | **Steam Rating:** 95% Positive

## 🎮 Core Concept

A roguelike where every action must be performed to the beat of the music. Move, attack, and dodge on a grid while maintaining rhythm. Combines musical skill with dungeon-crawling strategy.

## 🎵 Core Mechanic — Rhythm Grid Movement

- Move to the beat or lose your coin multiplier
- Enemies have distinct movement patterns per beat
- Learning enemy patterns is critical — each moves predictably
- Missing a beat = lose damage multiplier + miss your turn
- **Custom Music Support** — Import your own songs

## 🔫 Weapons & Items

- **Weapons:** Broadsword (3-tile cleave), Spear (2-tile range), Whip (3-tile range), Cat o' Nine Tails, Rapier (lunge)
- **Armor:** Leather, Chain, Plate, Glass, Obsidian
- **Spells:** Fireball, Freeze, Shield, Heal, Bomb, Earth
- **Rings:** Various passive bonuses

**Rhythm + Roguelike** — Entirely novel fusion. Music replaces turn timer. Created a whole subgenre (Cadence of Hyrule for Zelda).
